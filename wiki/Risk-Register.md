# Risk Register

## High Risks

| Risk | Severity | Response |
|---|---:|---|
| Looks like generic scanner | High | Keep upgrade-specific and ENS-named |
| ENS decorative | High | Product must require ENS records |
| Sourcify shallow | High | Use Sourcify data in verdict logic |
| False safety claims | High | Say verified is necessary, not sufficient |
| Demo too synthetic | Medium | Deploy real fixtures and verify them |
| Umia distracts | Medium | Keep Umia optional |

## Kill Conditions

- No live ENS resolution.
- No live Sourcify fetch.
- No chain read.
- No dangerous demo case.
- Verdict is only AI text.
- Pitch says generic scanner or AI auditor.

## Strong Signals

- Sourcify mentor sees the data used deeply.
- ENS mentor sees creative contract-map usage.
- Non-technical judge understands the red verdict immediately.
- Technical judge can inspect the evidence links.
